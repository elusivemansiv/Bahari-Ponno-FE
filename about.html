Our Story

From the Ancient Gardens of Sylhet

Our narrative begins in the lush estates of Sylhet and Panchagarh, where heritage meets quality. For generations, the mineral-rich soil and unique microclimates of Bangladesh have nurtured the world's most aromatic tea leaves.

At Bahari Ponno, we believe in preserving the artisanal methods of tea production. Every leaf is hand-picked at the peak of freshness, ensuring that the natural aroma and premium profile of our historic gardens are delivered directly to your cup.
